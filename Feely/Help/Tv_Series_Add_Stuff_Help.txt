Add data to TV Series :

You can add a new TV series
by filling in the appropriate 
fields.
Title : The original title
of the TV series.
Trailer : An internet link 
to the trailer of the TV series.
IMDB : The IMDB link, from 
http://www.imdb.com/
Genre : The type of the series.
Choose Mood : The mood that 
best suits the series.

Warning : You must correctly 
fill in all the fields!
