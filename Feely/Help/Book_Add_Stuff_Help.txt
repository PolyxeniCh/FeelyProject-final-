Add data to Books :

You can add a new book 
by filling in the 
appropriate fields.
Title : The original 
title of the book.
Author : The author 
of the book.
Link : An internet link 
to information about 
the book.
Genre : The type of 
the book.
Choose Mood : The mood 
that best suits the book.

Warning : You must correctly 
fill in all the fields!
