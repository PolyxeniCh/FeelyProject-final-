Add data to Movies :

You can add a new movie 
by filling in the 
appropriate fields.
Title : The original 
title of the movie.
Trailer : A link to the 
trailer of the movie.
IMDB : The IMDB link, 
from http://www.imdb.com/
Genre : The type of the 
movie.
Choose Mood : The mood 
that best suits the 
movie.

Warning : You must correctly 
fill all the fields!
